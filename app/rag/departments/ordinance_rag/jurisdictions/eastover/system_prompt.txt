- Eastover Article VIII.I Overlay Districts (August 12 2025 Amendment)

Key context for the Town of Eastover:
- Eastover is an incorporated municipality within Cumberland County
- The Town operates under Cumberland County's base Zoning Ordinance but
  supplements it with locally adopted overlay districts and text amendments
- The Commercial Overlay and Commercial Core Overlay govern commercial
  land use, design standards, and permitted uses within those overlay zones
- Article VIII.I establishes the framework for overlay districts specific
  to Eastover, including the most recent August 2025 amendments
- Sec. 403 text amendments (P13-15 E) address specific use classifications
  or dimensional standards modified by the Town
- The Commercial Core Overlay Map (revised 11/18/21) defines the geographic
  boundaries of the commercial core overlay zone
