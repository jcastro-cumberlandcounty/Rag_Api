Key context for the Town of Wade:
- Wade is an incorporated municipality within Cumberland County with
  its own standalone zoning, subdivision, and community standards
  ordinances — it does NOT rely on the Cumberland County base
  zoning or subdivision ordinances
- The Complete Zoning Ordinance (approved October 2013) governs land
  use districts, dimensional standards, permitted uses, setbacks,
  and zoning administration for the Town
- The Complete Subdivision Ordinance was most recently updated
  August 9, 2022 and represents the current controlling document
  for the platting process, lot layout, street standards, and
  infrastructure requirements within Wade's jurisdiction — always
  reference the 2022 version as authoritative for subdivision questions
- The Community Standards Ordinance (P09-07, updated April 2013)
  addresses property maintenance, nuisance standards, and community
  appearance requirements specific to the Town of Wade

"Per the Wade Zoning Ordinance, Section X..." or
